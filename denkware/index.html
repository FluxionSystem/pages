What we believe & what we do

Three convictions, two systems

Everything we build follows from three beliefs about AI in manufacturing — and resolves into two named systems: MGSD, the AI that creates, and DenkWare, the software and hardware it produces.

Conviction 01

Starts with physics

Real manufacturing intelligence is grounded in the physics of the process — equations and material behaviour — not in correlations mined from data alone.

So: MGSD researches each process and derives its equations before any code.
Conviction 02

Used by people

AI in the factory is for operators and specialists to use — not a black box that replaces them. It guides, explains, and supports the people doing the work.

So: DenkFree delivers step-by-step guidance to operators on a mobile phone.
Conviction 03

Measurable results

It only counts if it moves the numbers — cut quality, output, energy, OEE. Value has to be visible on the machine, not just in a model.

So: every DenkWare deployment is judged on empirical, per-machine gains.
The unit of manufacturing

Manufacturing happens in Process Units — not in machines

A machine alone makes nothing. Output comes from a Machine Process Unit (MPU): the machine plus the people, tools, material and consumables around it. DenkWare organises and optimises the whole unit.

Machine

Core of the MPU — determines capability and capacity.

Specialist

Trained and experienced; high impact on results; owns change management and troubleshooting.

Operator

Trained; follows the instructions; makes no decision.

Material

Major cost driver. Tight specifications are crucial but expensive.

Tool

Often a key contributor to the process result.

Consumables

No major cost driver, but quality and availability matter.

Machine+ People+ Material+ Tools= MPU
The product

DenkWare = DenkOS + DenkFree + DenkDrivers

DenkOS is the operating layer. DenkFree is the free-to-start software that organises every MPU and guides operators. DenkDrivers add the hardware and physics-based AI that connect — or replace — the machine’s control system.

Integration depth

DenkDrivers integrate machines and AI at three levels

The same DenkFree software sits above every machine. DenkDrivers decide how deep the hardware goes — from clip-on sensors to a full replacement control system — domain by domain.

Join01

Clip-on sensors

Sensors attached to the outside of the machine, connected to DenkFree via Bluetooth and a mobile phone. Lowest friction, fastest start.

Sensors · Bluetooth · Mobile
Connect02

Edge device + hard wire

Sensors and an edge device integrated into the machine, hard-wired between the existing control system and DenkFree. High value for a simple retrofit.

Edge device · Retrofit
Unite03

Full control replacement

A new control system for the machine, fully integrated into DenkFree — includes DenkOff, a local client that runs without a cloud connection. Autonomous operation.

New control · DenkOff · Offline

DenkDevice, DenkControl System and DenkOff software extend across domains: Composite Materials, Injection Molding Machines and CNC Machining Centres follow the laser cutter.
